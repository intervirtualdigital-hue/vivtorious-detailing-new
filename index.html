Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be present during the service?

Not necessarily. As long as we have access to your vehicle and a safe place to work, you can leave the keys and head inside. We'll text when we're on the way and when we're done.

How do I book?

Text us at (646) 371-3739 or DM @victoriousdetailing1. Send your address and a couple photos of the car — we'll lock in a slot.

Where do you operate?

NYC, Long Island, and the surrounding NY metro area. Outside that radius? Reach out anyway — sometimes we can make it work.

Are there any extra fees?

Only for heavy pet hair, sand, or stubborn stains. We always confirm with you before adding any charges. No surprises.

Do you charge a deposit?

No deposit required. You pay once the work is done.

What's the difference between an Interior Revival and an Exterior Master Class?

Interior Revival is everything inside — deep vacuum, steam, leather and fabric care. Exterior Master Class is everything outside — foam bath, hand wash, decon, and high-gloss finish. Most clients combine both. Full package menu coming soon.
